1 year in software development!

May 1, 2021

Some feelings:

  • Wow I am really here working as a developer!
  • Work flies by so fast everyday, which is such a pleasant feeling that things are going well and I am enjoying what I do!
  • Coding is still hard, but I am learning to be comfortably uncomfortable!
  • Some days I am John Snow - “You know nothing, John Snow.”
  • Some days I feel awesome, it was totally me, definitely not luck on solving that UI issue.
  • Need to study more on Kotlin, but it’s the weekend! YEP GOING TO ENJOY MY WEEKEND AND write about how awesome I am.

Reflections and Learnings:

“You don’t have to code everything out on your own.”
Before where I am now, I thought I am expected to code everything out on my own, be able to code features and make shit happen. Well, I still code things and make things happen. BUT with my awesome team’s help! And that’s totally ok!

“Communication is not easy."
I thought since I like to talk, I should be fine on reaching out to people/communications. I was wrong… How do I convey technical concepts, how do I convey what I tried and want to solve, how do I ask the right questions, how do I ask for help and understand what others are saying, how do I work with design and other engineers etc. I think I just need to be patient with myself and learn from experiences and others :)

“Slow down a bit for myself to absorb new learnings.”
Some things are hard, and I might ask for help to get the answers to solve a task. As I completed the task and move on, I think it’s important to make sure I understand or at least have a high level of understanding the solution or implementations.

Work in progress stuff:

  • Get better at completing tasks with less help as I learn more things!
  • Understand one small concept every week. (Could be something small, but I’d like to be able to explain it thoroughly)
  • Continue on getting help and helping others for an awesome team/work environment.
  • Be myself.
  • Try not to work more than 8 hours a day!
